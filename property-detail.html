About This Apartment

Welcome to The Signature Suite — HAVEN Lagos' flagship 3-bedroom apartment in the heart of Lekki Phase 1. Designed for guests who refuse to compromise on comfort or style, this apartment delivers a five-star experience in one of Lagos' most sought-after neighbourhoods.

The apartment spans 180 square metres across the 8th floor, with floor-to-ceiling windows that frame panoramic views of the Lagos skyline. The open-plan living and dining area is furnished with premium Italian pieces, while the kitchen is fully equipped for everything from a quick breakfast to a full dinner party.

All three bedrooms feature en-suite bathrooms, premium linen, and blackout curtains. The master suite includes a walk-in wardrobe and a rainfall shower. Guests have access to the building's rooftop pool, a fully equipped gym, and 24/7 concierge.

Check-in is seamless with our smart lock system — your unique code is sent to your phone upon booking confirmation. Our concierge team is available around the clock for airport pickup, grocery runs, restaurant reservations, and anything else you need.
