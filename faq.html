📅 Booking 7 questions

Booking is entirely online — no phone calls or DMs needed. Browse our apartments, select your check-in and check-out dates and number of guests, then proceed to our secure checkout. Choose your payment method (card, bank transfer or USSD), complete the payment, and you'll receive an instant confirmation email with your receipt and check-in details.

💡 The whole process takes less than 5 minutes.
Was this helpful?

You can complete a booking as a guest without creating an account. However, creating a free account gives you access to your booking history, receipts, and faster checkout on future bookings. We recommend signing up to keep all your stays in one place.

Was this helpful?

You can book up to 12 months in advance. Same-day bookings are also accepted, subject to availability. For same-day arrivals, we recommend booking at least 3 hours before your intended check-in to ensure everything is prepared for your arrival.

Was this helpful?

The minimum stay varies by apartment — it is clearly shown on each property page before you book. Most of our apartments have a 2-night minimum. Some premium properties require a 3-night minimum stay during peak periods such as public holidays and festive seasons.

Was this helpful?

Yes, date modifications are possible subject to availability. Contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference and the new dates you'd like. Modifications requested more than 48 hours before check-in are usually accommodated at no extra charge. Changes within 48 hours are treated as a cancellation and re-booking.

Was this helpful?

Yes — the moment your payment is confirmed, an automatic confirmation email is sent to the address you provided at checkout. This email contains your booking reference, property address, check-in details, smart lock access code, and a copy of your receipt.

💡 If you don't see it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. Still nothing? Contact us and we'll resend it immediately.
Was this helpful?

Yes. During checkout, you can enter the guest's name and contact details separately from the payment details. The booking confirmation and check-in instructions will be sent to whichever email address you specify.

💳 Payment 6 questions

Completely. All payments are processed through our encrypted secure checkout — never to a personal or unverified account. Every transaction is documented and an official receipt is issued instantly after payment. If you ever have concerns about a payment request, contact us directly before sending any money.

⚠️ HAVEN will never ask you to pay via personal bank accounts, cash on arrival, or informal transfers. If you receive such a request, it is not from us.
Was this helpful?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Debit or credit card (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Direct bank transfer to our verified GTBank account
  • USSD — dial *737* from any Nigerian bank line

All payment options are available at checkout. Choose whichever is most convenient for you.

Was this helpful?

Yes — an official receipt is sent to your email immediately after your payment is confirmed. The receipt includes the property name, your booking dates, the total amount paid, a unique booking reference number, and HAVEN's company details. This receipt is valid for expense claims and corporate reimbursement.

Was this helpful?

No. Before you pay, our checkout shows a complete price breakdown including the nightly rate, cleaning fee, and any service charges. There are no surprises after booking or on arrival. The total you see at checkout is exactly what you pay — nothing more.

Was this helpful?

Yes. We offer automatic discounts for longer stays. Weekly rates (7+ nights) are typically 15–20% lower than booking night by night. Monthly rates offer even greater savings. These discounted rates are shown automatically on the booking page when you select the relevant dates.

Was this helpful?

For standard short stays (under 14 nights), full payment is required at the time of booking. For extended stays of 14 nights or more, we offer a 50% deposit at booking with the balance due 7 days before check-in. Contact us directly to arrange extended stay payment terms.

🔑 Check-In & Your Stay 6 questions

Standard check-in is from 2:00 PM on your arrival date. Check-out is by 12:00 PM on your departure date. If you need an early check-in or late check-out, contact us at least 24 hours in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate you, subject to availability.

Was this helpful?

All HAVEN apartments use smart lock entry. When your booking is confirmed, a unique access code is sent to your email and phone. Simply enter the code on the door keypad at check-in. No key collection needed, no waiting for anyone to let you in.

💡 Your access code is only valid for the duration of your stay and deactivates automatically at check-out time.
Was this helpful?

Yes. Airport pickup is available from Murtala Muhammed International Airport (Lagos) and can be arranged through our concierge team. Pickup is typically arranged at a fixed rate and must be booked at least 12 hours before your flight arrival. Contact us via WhatsApp with your flight details to arrange this.

Was this helpful?

Our concierge team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week throughout your stay. You can reach us by WhatsApp, phone call, or email. Common requests we handle include restaurant recommendations, grocery delivery, local transport, maintenance issues, and anything else you need to make your stay comfortable.

Was this helpful?

Yes. All apartments are professionally cleaned and fully prepared before every check-in. For stays of 5 nights or more, a mid-stay cleaning is included at no extra charge. If you need additional cleaning during a shorter stay, this can be arranged through our concierge at a small additional fee.

Was this helpful?

All HAVEN apartments are equipped with 24-hour backup power — either a generator or inverter system. You will not lose power during your stay regardless of NEPA outages. AC, WiFi, kitchen appliances and all lighting remain fully operational at all times.

↩️ Cancellations & Refunds 4 questions

Our standard cancellation policy is:

  • 48+ hours before check-in: Full refund of the total amount paid
  • Within 48 hours of check-in: Non-refundable
  • No-show: Non-refundable

Some premium apartments or peak-period bookings may have different policies — these are always shown clearly on the property page before you pay.

Was this helpful?

To cancel, log into your account, go to "My Bookings" and click "Cancel Booking" next to the relevant reservation. Alternatively, contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference number and we will process the cancellation for you.

Was this helpful?

Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days of the cancellation being confirmed. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank or card provider — typically 3–7 additional days. If you haven't received your refund after 10 business days, contact us with your booking reference and we'll investigate immediately.

Was this helpful?

In the rare event that HAVEN needs to cancel a confirmed booking (for example, due to an unforeseen maintenance issue), you will receive a full refund within 24 hours and we will do everything possible to find you an alternative apartment of equal or higher standard at no extra cost. We will notify you as early as possible.

Was this helpful?
