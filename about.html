How It Started

A story every
Lagos traveller knows.

In 2022, our founder flew into Lagos for a two-week work project. He had booked what looked like a beautiful apartment in Lekki — photos were stunning, the host seemed legitimate, and the price was right. On arrival, the apartment looked nothing like the photos. No working AC. No WiFi. The door lock was broken.

He spent three hours trying to reach the host on WhatsApp before giving up and booking into a hotel at triple the price. That night, he asked one question: why is booking a shortlet in Lagos still this broken in 2022?

HAVEN was the answer. A platform where every apartment is physically verified, every booking is instant and receipted, and every guest has a real person they can call at 2am. No DMs, no guesswork, no stress.

"Lagos has some of the most beautiful apartments in Africa. Guests deserve to find and book them without the drama."

— Tunde Adesanya, Founder & CEO, HAVEN Lagos

What We Stand For

Our values aren't on a wall.
They're in every booking.

Every decision we make — from which apartments to list to how we handle a guest complaint — comes back to these six principles.

✅
Radical Honesty
We show every apartment exactly as it is. No filters, no flattering angles. If a property doesn't meet our standard, it doesn't get listed.
⚡
Speed as Respect
Your time is valuable. Instant confirmation, instant receipts, instant responses. We don't make guests wait for what they've already paid for.
🔐
Security First
Every transaction is encrypted and receipted. We never ask you to pay to a personal account. Every naira is accounted for.

Guest-First Always

Built for Lagos
